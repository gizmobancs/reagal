Our Heritage

Back to About Us

Reagal Events stands on a foundation shaped by generations of circus tradition, carried forward by its co-founders Carolyn Roberts and Paul Rosairere, whose families have long been woven into the fabric of British circus culture. As a company, Reagal Events continues this legacy by presenting family‑focused touring productions across the UK, celebrating both classic big‑top spectacle and modern live entertainment . Their connection to The Wonder Circus further reflects this heritage, as the show embraces the essence of a traditional family circus while presenting it in a contemporary, vibrant style that resonates with today’s audiences . This lineage echoes the broader history of British circus, which traces its roots to the pioneering work of Philip and Patty Astley in the 18th century—figures widely credited with shaping the modern circus through their innovative blend of equestrian performance, acrobatics, and theatrical flair . Reagal Events carries this spirit forward, honouring the artistry, dedication, and showmanship that have defined circus families for centuries. Through their touring productions, seasonal shows, and commitment to preserving the magic of live performance, they continue to share the wonder, excitement, and community that have always been at the heart of circus tradition.
