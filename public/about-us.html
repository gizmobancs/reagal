About Us

Get to know the people, heritage, and behind-the-scenes story of Reagal Events.

Who We Are

Reagal Events is built on generations of circus tradition, carried forward by co-founders Carolyn Roberts and Paul Rosairerere, whose families have been part of Britain’s travelling show heritage for over a century. What began as two family legacies—rooted in horsemanship, showmanship, and life on the road—has grown into a modern events company that still honours the magic of its origins.

Our Heritage

Reagal Events stands on a foundation shaped by generations of circus tradition, carried forward by its co-founders Carolyn Roberts and Paul Rosairerere, whose families have long been woven into the fabric of British circus culture. As a company, Reagal Events continues this legacy by presenting family‑focused touring productions across the UK, celebrating both classic big‑top spectacle and modern live entertainment .

Our Beautiful Animals

Carolyn Roberts has become widely admired in the circus world for her remarkable partnership with horses, a bond shaped by a lifetime spent within a multigenerational show‑family tradition. Her approach blends classical horsemanship with the warmth and personality of a true ring entertainer, creating acts that feel both timeless and deeply personal.

Life on the Road

Life on the road as a circus family is a rhythm all its own—a blend of tradition, teamwork, and adventure that few other lifestyles can match. Each season brings a new landscape as the big top rises in towns across the country, turning empty fields into vibrant hubs of colour, music, and community.

Our Trucks and Transport

Circus transport vehicles are far more than a way to get from one town to the next—they’re the moving heartbeat of a travelling show. For generations, circuses have relied on long convoys of brightly painted wagons, living trailers, animal transporters, and equipment trucks, each one packed with the tools, props, and personalities that bring the big top to life.
