About Us

Who We Are

Reagal Events is built on generations of travelling-show tradition, brought to life by our founders and a close-knit team who live and breathe the circus. Rooted in horsemanship, showmanship, and life on the road, we create modern family entertainment that still honours the magic of its origins.

Our Heritage

Reagal Events stands on a foundation shaped by generations of British circus tradition. We’re proud to carry forward the craft, care, and community spirit of the travelling show—celebrating the past while presenting it in a fresh, modern way for today’s audiences.

Our Beautiful Animals

We pride ourselves on presenting some of the very best horse and animal acts from around the UK. Every routine is built on trust, patience, and top-level care—so what you see in the ring is a calm partnership, a polished performance, and animals that are treated with respect every step of the way.

Life on the Road
