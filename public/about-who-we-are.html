Who We Are

Back to About Us

Reagal Events is built on generations of circus tradition, carried forward by co-founders Carolyn Roberts and Paul Rosaire, whose families have been part of Britain’s travelling show heritage for over a century. What began as two family legacies—rooted in horsemanship, showmanship, and life on the road—has grown into a modern events company that still honours the magic of its origins. We believe in creating experiences that bring people together, just as the circus once gathered whole communities under the big top. From our touring productions to our seasonal shows, every event we create is shaped by the values we were raised with: hard work, authenticity, and a deep love for live entertainment. Reagal Events is more than a company; it’s a continuation of a living tradition, proudly sharing the wonder, warmth, and artistry of our circus heritage with audiences today.
