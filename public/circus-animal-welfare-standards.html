Our commitment

Reagal Events is committed to responsible animal care. Welfare is not a single action — it’s a daily standard that covers housing, feeding, enrichment, veterinary support, transport planning, and staff training.

Daily care and routine

Animals require consistent routines. This includes fresh water, appropriate feed, clean environments, and attentive handling. We also focus on calm, predictable processes during travel and set-up so animals remain settled.

Transport and touring

Touring requires planning to reduce stress: sensible travel times, safe and secure transport, and appropriate rest once on site. We prioritise safety for animals, staff, and the public.

Transparency

If you have questions, we welcome respectful enquiries. You can also learn more about our team and heritage on the About Us page.
