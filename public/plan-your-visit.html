Plan Your Visit

Everything you need to know before you arrive — timings, seating, accessibility, and family-friendly tips to make your day at Reagal Events as smooth and magical as possible.

Quick Essentials

Arrive 20–30 mins early Family-friendly atmosphere Touring UK towns Licensed animal welfare

How long is the show?

the average runtime is 1.5 - 2 hours. Show length can vary slightly by programme and season , but performances are designed to be enjoyable for families — including children — with a clear start time and a comfortable pace throughout. Your ticket page for your town will always show the performance time.

For all upcoming dates and times, see the General Tour page.

When should we arrive?

We recommend arriving 30–40 minutes before showtime so you can park, find your seats, and settle in. If you arrive right at the start time, you may miss the opening moments while seating is completed.

Seating & family comfort

Our shows are designed for families and first-time visitors as well as seasoned circus fans. If you are attending with young children, consider bringing a light layer for warmth and anything small that helps them stay comfortable during the performance.

Accessibility

We aim to make Reagal Events welcoming and accessible. If you have accessibility needs, please contact us in advance via the Contact page so we can advise on the best arrival time and any venue-specific details.

Parking & finding the venue

Each town page includes the location and booking link for that venue. Visit Tour Locations and select your town to see dates, times, and the TicketSource booking link.

Animal welfare & standards

Animal welfare is central to what we do. You can read our standards and licensing information on our dedicated page: Circus Animal Welfare Standards.

FAQs

Is the circus suitable for young children?

Yes — our performances are designed to be family-friendly. If you have very young children, arriving early and choosing a comfortable seating spot can help.

Where do I buy tickets?

Tickets are available on the General Tour page and on individual town pages.

Do you tour year-round?

We tour across the year with seasonal highlights. Check All Shows for current listings.

Can I contact you with a question about my visit?

Absolutely — use the Contact page and we’ll get back to you as soon as possible.
